## Mining Strategies on MineMore

**Brian:** Nice. Same here. Now on MineMore -- you don't have to tell me anything strategy-wise you don't want to, but what kind of strategy are you running? Are you finding yourself with positive PnL?

And I run basically -- for me, the thing that gives me peace of mind, helps me sleep at night and is doing really well -- I've got five different mines going, all relatively low in terms of cost allocation per round.
